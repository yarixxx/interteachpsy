List of the books in the Teaching Psychological Science Series

The books include: ideas for beginning the course; sample lecture outlines for the entire course; examples and applications that link the course content to everyday student experience; classroom demonstrations and activities with an emphasis on promoting active learning and critical thinking; discussion of sensitive and difficult-to-teach topics and ethical issues likely to be encountered throughout the semester; course-specific options for evaluating student performance; a chapter on available resources for teaching the course.
